Frequently Asked Questions What is this app for? Purpose This app is designed to help you track your daily steps, distance walked, during your walks, runs, or other physical activities. How does the app work? How it works The app uses your smartphone's built-in sensors to track your movements and calculate the distance you've walked or run. It also uses weather data to record the temperature during your activities. Do I need to create an account to use the app? Account Creation Yes, you need to create an account to use the app. Creating an account allows you to store and view your activity history and set personalized goals. Is my data secure? Security Yes, we take the security and privacy of your data very seriously. We use industry-standard encryption and secure servers to protect your data. Can I use the app offline? Offline Mode Yes, you can use the app offline. However, you won't be able to access some features, such as syncing your data to the cloud or getting weather updates. Can I use the app to track other physical activities, such as cycling or swimming? Other Uses Currently, the app is designed to track only steps and distance walked or run.
